Howdy user! Curious about what this is and does? Read on!

WHAT

Monitor the weather in different locations. Set "low" and "high" temperature alerts, be reminded if the temperature goes below or above those thresholds. How exciting!

HOW

Locations are displayed as cards, you can poke, rub and drag them.

There is only one way to do everything! One way to add, remove, move or modify, and you can only do one thing at a time.

Everything is animated. It's slow and soothing, since this isn't a productiviy app. You're supposed to be poking it when you're thinking about taking some time off. Slow living, folks!

The palette is reduced to four colours and five shades of gray. One font family is used. No raster images (except for favicons). Easy on the eyes and your phone bill.

Speaking of phones, it should work and look good on yours. Save it to your home screen!

Last but not least: everything is synced, real-time. Pop open two of these babies on different devices, log in as the same user (or anonymously) and see the magic. Thanks, Firebase!

WHY

This is a job application. Pun intended.

I also needed to get familiar with a modern front-end framework/library, ES6, webpack and s'more things.

I took my time.


ONE MORE THING...

Weather and location data is provided by OpenWeatherMap, because their API gives you the most with a free account. But this free account either works or it doesn't, and even when it does, it doesn't always.
